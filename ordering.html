Step 1:

What drink do you want?

There are usually different types of drinks, ranging from milk teas to cream floats to fruit teas and more.

Do you want your drink hot or cold?

Only some drinks can be made hot whereas almost all drinks can be made cold.

Step 3:

What size drink do you want?

Usually cold drinks come in sizes medium and large and hot drinks are only one size.

Step 4:

What toppings do you want? (Optional)

There are various toppings depending on the store you go to, such as tapioca, jelly, popping bubbles, syrup, pudding, etc.

Step 5:

How much ice do you want?

You can adjust the amount of ice you want in your drink.

Step 6:

How much sugar do you want?

You can adjust the amount of sugar you want in your drink.
