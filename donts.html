Don't #1:

It is highly recommended you DO NOT stab the drink with the flat end of the straw.

Please use the pointy end of the straw.

Don't #2:

It is highly recommended you DO NOT peel off the plastic film on your drink.

Please leave the plastic film attached.

Don't #3:

It is highly recommended you DO NOT consume your drink and toppings with a spoon.

Please consume your drink with a straw.
